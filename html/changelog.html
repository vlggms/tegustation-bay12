Ilysen updated:

  • You can now deconstruct nearly-empty or destroyed gas canisters with a welding tool.
  • You can now repair damage to gas canisters with duct tape.
  • Examining a gas canisters now gives a rough idea of how damaged it is.
  • Improved the feedback for attacking canisters - it now has a normal attack animation and plays a sound.
  • By making canisters rupture at zero health instead of 10, they're technically slightly stronger now!
  • Updated the codex entry for canisters to reflect new functionalities.
  • Renamed canisters - now they're `gas canister` instead of `Canister`. Also added a simple examine description.
  • Helium, Methyl Bromide, and Chlorine canisters now explicitly state what they contain on their label instead of having a chemical formula - this is to bring them in line with other gas canisters.
  • The chef's cookers (griddle, oven, etc.) have received a UI facelift.
  • Cookers now properly play a ding when they finish cooking - previously the sound played at 0.5% volume.
  • Adherents now have a special cell, called the "crystal-ceramic grid", instead of a standard superior power cell. It is mechanically identical, but shows instead of "superior power cell" when you recharge from an electron reservoir.
  • The electron reservoir now has first-person messages towards the person using it. Zap.
  • Slamming someone into the electron reservoir now drops the grab, since the victim goes flying across the room.

Ilysen updated:

  • Rewrote the in-game Changelog popup to mention TeguStation instead of Baystation 12. Lots of people from the Baystation 12 dev team have been added to the credits as a result.
  • Bioprinters and prosthetic organ fabricators have a new interface, based in NanoUI, instead of using a basic HTML popup.

  • Added feedback for when an organ printer starts creating an organ.
  • Injecting blood samples into a bioprinter now actually removes the blood sample from the syringe.
  • Prosthetic organ fabricators have been renamed to prostheses fabricators, in an effort to clarify that they can print robotic limbs as well as robotic organs.
